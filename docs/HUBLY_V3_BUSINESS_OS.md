# Hubly v3 — Business Operating System

**Status:** Product constitution (Milestone 3)  
**Supersedes:** “AI onboarding as the product” (Milestone 2 Home / Daily / report chrome as primary)

---

## Philosophy

Hubly is **not** an AI onboarding app.

Hubly is a **business operating system**.

| Role | Job |
|------|-----|
| **AI** | Build and continuously improve the business |
| **Hubly** | Run the business |

Think:

- Cursor builds code.
- VS Code is where you work.

**AI should generate. Hubly should operate.**

---

## Two modes only

### Mode 1 — Create (Business Creation Mode)

The AI is building your business.

- Ask intelligent questions (conversation, not forms)
- Website, booking, packages, brand, gallery update **live**
- Customer watches the business appear

Journey:

```
Landing → Creative Director (conversation) → Reveal → Continue
```

### Mode 2 — Operate

The moment they click **Continue to My Workspace**, Hubly becomes the OS they already have:

Website · Booking · CRM · Customers · Jobs · Calendar · Revenue · Messages · Marketplace · Payments

AI sits **above** every feature as a silent employee — conversation-first improvements, not report pages.

---

## Reveal (Create exit)

Show:

> Your business is ready.

Visual cards only:

- Website
- Booking
- CRM
- Packages
- Calendar

**Continue →** enters Operate (existing dashboard = Home).

Remove from primary Reveal:

Business Summary · Business Confidence · Timeline · Certificate · Launch Summary · Morning Brief · Alternatives · Reports

---

## Operate Home (dashboard)

Every login answers three questions instantly:

1. What does my business look like?
2. What’s happening today?
3. What should I do next?

**Website preview is the hero** — render it, don’t describe it.

One recommendation at a time. Apply / Dismiss.

AI reports (confidence, evolution, forecast, archives, long summaries) live behind **Learn more** — never on Home.

---

## AI rules

- Show, don’t tell.
- Visual before text.
- AI builds in the background.
- One sentence of explanation after showing the result.
- Replace editors with conversations where possible.
- Reduce copy by ≥70% vs Milestone 2 report surfaces.
- Every screen helps get customers, book jobs, or grow revenue.

---

## Chassis rule

Do **not** replace CRM, Jobs, Revenue, Customers, Quotes, Calendar, Website, Messaging, Marketplace, or Payments.

Those are the operating system. AI is the differentiator **above** them — not the destination.
