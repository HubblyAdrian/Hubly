# Hubly v1.0 — First 100 Customers

> **What must be true before we can charge our first 100 customers?**

---

## North Star

**Revenue generated through Hubly-powered businesses.**

### Three questions that define success

1. **Can a business launch?**  
2. **Can a customer pay?**  
3. **Can the owner run their business every day without leaving Hubly?**  

When those answers are yes, AI marketing and autonomous growth multiply value — they are not prerequisites.

---

## Required for v1

| Outcome | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Business Created | Conversation creates the company |
| Website live | Publish without manual rescue |
| Hire transaction | Discover → book → pay → confirm → job in Hubly |
| Owner loop | Notify → accept → calendar → CRM → complete → review |
| Owner Feed | Chronological Hubly activity visible on login |
| Hubly Daily | Advice-first morning briefing |
| Revenue loop | Lead → book → pay → job → complete → review → repeat |

---

## Not required for v1

AI Marketing automation · Autonomous Growth · Ads publishing · Multi-location · Advanced analytics · Accounting · Multi-registrar domains

---

## Overbuilding risk

Architecture is mature. Roadmap is clear.

Do not expand scope. Close First Customer with a **real production payment**, then Business Running.
Measure customer outcomes, not feature completion.
